Aerial Schools New York City

New York City is lucky to have many great aerial schools. There are also aerial dance companies and aerialists who teach independently. There are too many independent aerialists in the city for me to list them all here, but as you grow in your practice, don't forget to inquire about training with aerialists whose style you gravitate toward.
