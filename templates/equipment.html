Buying Basic Aerial Equipment

Aerial equipment ranges from the relatively straight-forward to the incredibly complicated. Here are a few places where you can begin to build your own aerial arsenal.

REMEMBER! Rigging without proper knowledge can be life threatening. Talk to an experienced aerialist and/or professional rigger before getting or attempting to rig your own equipment.

  • Custom Built Equipment
    www.cbe-circus.com
    CBE sells several different types of aerial apparatuses (including rope, lyra, silks, straps, trapeze) and rigging.

  • Fabric Depot
    www.fabricdepotco.com
    Many aerialists buy their aerial fabric (aerial silks) here. See page 31 of the current catalog for the specific fabric.

  • Mutual Hardware
    www.mutualhardware.com
    Mutual Hardware is located in Long Island City, Queens and sells many of the commonly needed pieces of rigging hardware.
